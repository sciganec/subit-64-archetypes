## üßò PART ONE: PERSONAL DEVELOPMENT

### Self-Diagnosis

The foundation of personal work with SUBIT is accurate self-diagnosis. At any moment, ask:

| Question | Options | Reflection |
|----------|---------|------------|
| **Who am I now?** | ME / WE / YOU / THEY | Am I acting alone? In group? For others? As witness? |
| **Where am I now?** | EAST / SOUTH / WEST / NORTH | Am I beginning? In full activity? Completing? Resting? |
| **When am I now?** | SPRING / SUMMER / AUTUMN / WINTER | Is this a time of birth? Peak? Harvest? Stillness? |

**Exercise:** Three times daily (morning, midday, evening), record your state:

```
Date: ________
Time: ________

Current state: [___, ___, ___] ‚Äî [archetype name]

What led to this state?
_________________________________________________

What would be a desirable next state?
_________________________________________________
```

### Identifying Patterns

After a week of self-diagnosis, review your data:

- Which archetypes appear most frequently?
- Are you stuck in certain states?
- Do you cycle through states in a predictable pattern?
- Which states are absent from your experience?

**Patterns to watch for:**

| Pattern | Meaning | Intervention |
|---------|---------|--------------|
| **Fixation** | Same state repeatedly | Seek impulse and catalyst for transmutation |
| **Avoidance** | Certain states never appear | Explore why; invite those energies |
| **Rapid cycling** | Changing too quickly | Practice staying with difficult states |
| **Balanced cycle** | Healthy range of states | Maintain; deepen practice |

### The Daily Transmutation Practice

**Morning (5 minutes):**

1. **Diagnose** your current state
2. **Choose** a target state for the day
3. **Identify** needed impulse and catalyst
4. **Set intention** aloud

Example:
```
Current: [ME, NORTH, WINTER] ‚Äî Anchoret (too isolated)
Target: [WE, SOUTH, SUMMER] ‚Äî Conciliar (connection)

Impulse needed: [THEY, EAST, SPRING] ‚Äî Ghost (unexpected invitation)
Catalyst needed: [YOU, WEST, AUTUMN] ‚Äî Confessor (someone who listens)

Intention: "Today I embody the Anchoret. I open myself to the Ghost 
as impulse and the Confessor as catalyst, that I may transmute to Conciliar."
```

**Evening (5 minutes):**

1. **Review** what occurred
2. **Record** the actual transmutation (or lack thereof)
3. **Reflect** on what helped or hindered
4. **Give thanks** to the archetypes present

Example:
```
What happened: A friend called unexpectedly (Ghost). We talked deeply (Confessor).
I felt genuine connection by evening (Conciliar achieved).

Transmutation: ‚úì Complete

If I could live today again: I would answer the phone sooner.
```

```
Recluse ‚äï Egregore (00 11 11) ‚äï Therapist (01 11 00) = Shared Experience

### The Transmutation Interview

A structured therapeutic conversation:

1. **Current State:** "What archetype best describes where you are right now?"
2. **Target State:** "Where would you like to be?"
3. **Impulse:** "What external event or energy might help you move?"
4. **Catalyst:** "Who or what could facilitate this movement?"
5. **History:** "Have you been here before? What helped then?"
6. **Resources:** "What archetypes do you have access to?"
7. **Plan:** "What's one small step toward the needed impulse?"

### Group Therapy Applications

5. Members then move to stand with their desired archetype
