### Example 1: Career Transition

**Situation:** Maria, 34, has worked as a corporate lawyer for 10 years. She feels burned out and disconnected from her work, but is afraid to leave the security of her position.

**Diagnosis:**

| Dimension | State | Archetype |
|-----------|-------|-----------|

**Formula:**
```
Judge (10 01 01) ‚äï Ghost (00 10 10) ‚äï Confessor (01 01 01) = Pioneer (10 10 10)
```

**Interpretation:** To move from judgment to new beginning, Maria needs:
- **Ghost:** An unexpected impulse from outside ‚Äî perhaps a chance encounter, an unexpected opportunity, a sudden insight.

**Outcome:** Maria attended a weekend workshop (unexpected invitation = Ghost) where she met a career counselor who simply listened to her story without offering advice (Confessor). Within three months, she had left law and started a nonprofit (Pioneer).

**Reflection:** The transmutation required both external impulse (the workshop invitation) and relational catalyst (the counselor's presence). Neither alone would have been sufficient.

### Example 22: Community Organizing

**Situation:** A neighborhood is facing gentrification. Long-time residents are being displaced. A community organizer wants to build collective power.

**Diagnosis (community archetype):**

| Group | Archetype |
|-------|-----------|

**Community archetype:** Guardians ‚äï Pioneers ‚äï Storm = ?

Guardians ‚äï Pioneers = (11 10 00) ‚äï (11 10 10) = 00 00 10 ‚Äî Potential
